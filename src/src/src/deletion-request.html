DIASPORAID PROVIDES YOU WITH THE MEANS TO DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT AND ALL THE RELATED INFORMATION (PROFILE, POSTS, COMMENTS, ROLES).

1. How can I delete my account and/or related content?

To delete your account and miscellaneous related items - such as your posts, comments, products, services and franchises, send us a request to accounts@diasporaID.com.

Indicate your name, your hometown and residence city currently set in the app. If you used an email address / phone number to register, we will need those to process your request. If, alternatively, you registered using a social / third party provider (e.g. Facebook, Apple, LinkedIn, Gmail) please indicate that as well.

2. What will be deleted exactly? Will you still have any stored information?

We do not store any information about you outside your profile on the application. More importantly, your phone number, email address, password and any account identifier are encrypted and handled by Microsoft's B2C identity service - we do not store any sensitive information on our servers. All of your information is encrypted, and will be deleted when your request is fully processed.

After processing, the following will be deleted:

  • Your profile and all related information (About, Biography, Media Gallery)
  • Your account (email address / phone number used to register)
  • Your associations (with other profiles, pages, diplomatic missions, towns etc.)
  • Your comments, posts, events, likes, products, services, franchises and media items

Note, however, that any organization/company/startup that you created will not be deleted.

3. How long will processing my request take?

Due to the number of associations we have to process, your account deletion request will take between 30 to 45 days to be fully processed. You will be informed once it is complete.


If at any time you have any further inquiries or concerns, you can email us on info@diasporaid.com and we will do our best to answer your inquiry at the earliest.
